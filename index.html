About InfinityWorkup

InfinityWorkup is a workout that pushes boundaries and redefines what it means to embrace chaos. Dive into a world where hell meets artistry, and every detail is crafted to perfection.

Workout Versions

Version 1: With Push-Ups

1. Beginner (The First Circle)

Work: 45 seconds per exercise

Rest: 15 seconds between exercises

Rounds: 3

Rest between rounds: 1 minute

  • Pistol Squats with Pulse (5-second descent, 3 pulses at the bottom, 5-second ascent)
  • Push-Ups with Shoulder Tap Hold (hold each shoulder tap for 2 seconds)
  • Jumping Lunges with Isometric Hold (hold the bottom of each lunge for 5 seconds)
  • Spiderman Push-Ups (knee to elbow on each rep, hold for 2 seconds)
  • Single-Leg Weighted Glute Bridges with Pulse (10-second hold at the top, then 3 pulses)

2. Normal (The Second Circle)

Work: 60 seconds per exercise

Rest: 15 seconds between exercises

Rounds: 4

Rest between rounds: 1 minute

  • Pistol Squats with Double Pulse (5-second descent, 6 pulses at the bottom, 5-second ascent)
  • Archer Push-Ups (alternate sides, hold for 2 seconds at the bottom)
  • Jumping Lunges with Double Isometric Hold (hold the bottom of each lunge for 10 seconds)
  • Spiderman Push-Ups with Reach-Through (reach under your torso on each rep)
  • Single-Leg Weighted Glute Bridges with Double Pulse (20-second hold at the top, then 6 pulses)

3. Advanced (The Third Circle)

Work: 75 seconds per exercise

Rest: 15 seconds between exercises

Rounds: 5

Rest between rounds: 1 minute

  • Pistol Squats with Triple Pulse (5-second descent, 9 pulses at the bottom, 5-second ascent)
  • Plyometric Push-Ups (explode off the ground, hold landing for 2 seconds)
  • Jumping Lunges with Triple Isometric Hold (hold the bottom of each lunge for 15 seconds)
  • Spiderman Push-Ups with Rotation (rotate into a side plank on each rep)
  • Single-Leg Weighted Glute Bridges with Triple Pulse (30-second hold at the top, then 9 pulses)

4. Hard (The Fourth Circle)

Work: 90 seconds per exercise

Rest: 15 seconds between exercises

Rounds: 6

Rest between rounds: 1 minute

  • Pistol Squats with Quad Pulse (5-second descent, 12 pulses at the bottom, 5-second ascent)
  • One-Arm Push-Ups (Assisted) (use your other hand for support, hold for 2 seconds at the bottom)
  • Jumping Lunges with Quad Isometric Hold (hold the bottom of each lunge for 20 seconds)
  • Spiderman Push-Ups with Leg Lift (lift your leg in the air during the side plank rotation)
  • Single-Leg Weighted Glute Bridges with Quad Pulse (40-second hold at the top, then 12 pulses)

5. Impossible (The Fifth Circle)

Work: 120 seconds per exercise

Rest: 15 seconds between exercises

Rounds: 7

Rest between rounds: 1 minute

  • Pistol Squats with Quintuple Pulse (5-second descent, 15 pulses at the bottom, 5-second ascent)
  • One-Arm Push-Ups (Unassisted) (hold for 3 seconds at the bottom)
  • Jumping Lunges with Quintuple Isometric Hold (hold the bottom of each lunge for 25 seconds)
  • Spiderman Push-Ups with Leg Lift and Hop (add a hop when transitioning between sides)
  • Single-Leg Weighted Glute Bridges with Quintuple Pulse (50-second hold at the top, then 15 pulses)
